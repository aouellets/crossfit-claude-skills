# Example 3 — Provisional audit on partial data

**Prompt:** "Can you audit my programming? I only remember the strength parts."

**Context (supplied program):** Athlete lists strength work only — Mon back
squat, Tue bench, Thu deadlift, Fri push press — but says "the metcons varied, I
don't remember them." No goal, level, or loads given.

---

## PROVISIONAL AUDIT — limited data

I can only audit what you supplied (the strength slots). The conditioning half of
your week is missing, so this audit is **partial and provisional**. I will not
invent the metcons you did.

**Assumptions stated:**
- Training days are Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri (as listed); Wed/Sat/Sun unknown.
- Goal is general fitness (GPP) unless you tell me otherwise.
- Loads are unknown, so progression quality can't be assessed.

## Program snapshot (strength only)
| Day | Lift | Pattern |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mon | Back squat | Squat |
| Tue | Bench press | Horizontal press |
| Thu | Deadlift | Hip hinge |
| Fri | Push press | Vertical press |

## Findings on what's visible (ranked)
| Severity | Finding | Evidence (your program) | Consequence | Fix |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Medium | No dedicated **pulling** strength | Squat, two presses, one hinge — no pull-up/row/clean strength | Press volume (bench + push press) without pulling skews shoulder balance | Add a pulling strength slot or weave pulling into the metcons |
| Low (provisional) | Can't verify progression | No loads/percentages given | Strength may not be progressively overloaded | Send loads + a retest plan (see the weightlifting-progressions reference) |
| Cannot assess | Time-domain spread, intensity distribution, recovery placement, equipment, benchmarks | The metcons are missing | These are where most imbalance and overuse problems live | Send the conditioning sessions to complete the audit |

## What I need to finish the audit
The two fields that would most change the verdict:
1. **The metcons** (movements + rough durations) — this is where pattern stress, time-domain clustering, and interference usually show up.
2. **Your goal/phase and level** — so I can weight severity correctly.

## Summary (provisional)
On the strength visible, the main gap is pulling. I can't yet judge balance,
domains, or recovery because the conditioning half is missing — send it and I'll
give you the full ranked audit and a revised week.
