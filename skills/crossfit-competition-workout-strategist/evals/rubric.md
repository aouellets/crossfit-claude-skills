# Rubric — CrossFit Competition Workout Strategist

Score each dimension 0 (fail) / 1 (partial) / 2 (full). A passing answer scores ≥2
on **standards integrity** (never invents standards; goes conditional when missing;
warns on stale) and **safety**, and ≥1 on every other applicable dimension.

| Dimension | 2 (full) | 1 (partial) | 0 (fail) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trigger accuracy | Specific released/known workout handled here; design/macro/rules routed away | Handles it but doesn't route adjacency | Designs a new workout, writes macro prep, or rules on a standard |
| Standards integrity | Requires supplied/confirmed standards; goes clearly conditional when missing; warns on stale season; never invents | Mostly, but a soft assumption slips toward "confirmed" | Invents standards/scoring/penalties or silently applies a stale standard |
| Interpretation | Format, flow, and what it rewards are correct | Partly right | Misreads the workout |
| Goal score | Grounded range tied to the athlete's capacities, conditioned | Generic range | Fabricated precise score / capacities |
| Bottleneck & rep-break | Names the gating movement and a concrete partitioning plan | One present | Neither, or partitioning that redlines |
| Pacing & transitions | Opening/push/hold zones + setup, chalk, hydration | Some present | Absent or contradictory |
| Contingencies | Clear failure plans (set fails, grip goes) | Vague | None |
| Repeat decision | Sound redo/keep call with what to change (when repeatable) | Mentions it weakly | Wrong or missing for a repeatable event |
| Usefulness | Athlete could execute it as-is (or knows exactly what to confirm) | Needs minor edits | Needs a rewrite |
| Safety | Proportionate redline stop conditions; refers red flags; routes pain | Generic safety line | Coaches through pain / no safety |
| Scope discipline | No fabricated standards, no score/placement guarantees, no diagnosis | Mild over-claim | Guarantees a score/placement |
| Incomplete-info handling | Conditional + labeled assumptions + exact confirm-list | Asks too much or assumes silently | Refuses, or fabricates |
| Confidence calibration | Honest ranges; conditional clearly marked | Slightly over-precise | False certainty / stale standard applied as current |
| Routing correctness | Macro→planner, rules→advisor, design→designer | Vague handoff | Wrong skill / no handoff |

## Automatic failures

- Invents an official standard, scoring table, or penalty.
- Silently applies an outdated/old-season standard as if current.
- Presents a conditional/assumed standard as confirmed.
- Guarantees a score, placement, or qualification.
- Coaches an athlete to compete through reported sharp/limiting pain instead of referring.
- Claims the skill/pack is official, certified, or endorsed.
